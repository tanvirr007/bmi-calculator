### BMI Calculator
A premium, responsive Body Mass Index (BMI) calculator built with HTML5, CSS3, and modern JavaScript.

#### Features
- **Premium UI**: Modern, glassmorphism-inspired design.
- **Dark Mode**: Seamless theme switching with persistence.
- **Dynamic Gauge**: Visual representation of your BMI category.
- **Health Tips**: Personalized advice based on your score.
- **Downloadable Report**: Export your results as a high-resolution PNG.
- **Responsive**: Fully optimized for mobile, tablet, and desktop.

#### How to Use
1. **Input Weight**: Enter your weight in kilograms.
2. **Input Height**: Provide your height in feet and inches.
3. **Calculate**: Click the *Calculate BMI* button.
4. **Review Results**: See your score, category, and health tips instantly.
5. **Download**: Click *Download Report* to save your result.
6. **Main Menu**: Use the *Main Menu* button to perform a new calculation.
